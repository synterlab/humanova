About Humanova

A library built by practitioners, for practitioners. No fluff. No paywalls. Just tools that work.

"Grounded in innovation theory, design thinking, and social development practice. Designed for the day you actually need it."

What is Humanova?

Humanova is a DIY toolkit platform for creative industry practitioners and NGOs. Every toolkit is practical, field-tested, and free to download, use, and adapt for your context.

It was built on a simple observation: practitioners often know what they need to do, but lack structured tools to do it well. Humanova closes that gap.

Running a community workshop? Designing a new service? Building a funding case? There is a toolkit here for exactly that moment.
